Start a Chapter

Creating a chapter, either middle or high school chapter, will help bring Project BEST to more places and allows it to impact more students. Project BEST is what it is thanks to it's chapters, both middle and high school ones. Starting a chapter is the best way to get involved! We will help you through the entire process and provide all the materials necessary for you to be successful!

Project BEST Middle School Chapters are designed with the philosophy of creating curiosity in students. Our chapters are run by trained members of Project BEST. If you’re interested in creating a Project BEST Middle School Chapter (either student or school representative), please let us know (write "Middle School Chapter" in the subject and the school's name in the message)! We do most of the work in training and arranging for the chapter to be set up! You’ll have all the resources necessary to be successful!

Our Current Chapters: Lake Braddock Secondary School, Rocky Run Middle School, Robinson Secondary School, Robert Frost Middle School, Walt Whitman Middle School, Poe Middle School, Franklin Middle School, Irving Middle School, Greenbrier Middle School, Hickory Middle School, and the STEP Tutoring Center.

Volunteer

We're constantly looking for volunteers who can help out at our chapters or sessions! Scroll down to learn more about volunteering opportunities and to join our volunteering email list.

There are currently no open volunteer opportunities. Please check back soon for more opportunities, or join our email list!

Make sure to join our email list by sending a message. Just leave us with your name, your email and “Volunteer” as the subject line. Feel free to add any extra information in the message (school, grade level if a student).

As a volunteer, you will be running booths, sessions, and overall helping a program run smoothly. Every opportunity is different and we promise it will be fun and rewarding! Being a dedicated volunteer can result in awards from Project BEST as well as recognition from your local community.

Project BEST also signs off on NHS and other volunteering hours!

Host an Event

Are you looking to hold a STEM event in your area? We're constantly looking for places to hold Project BEST events, and we would love to come host a session at your location!

One key part of Project BEST is our community centric events, and we are constantly planning both large and small ones. Our events range from large STEM events, Business and Research Symposiums, and even small, local sessions at libraries and community centers!

Interested in bringing a Project BEST event to your community? Then please let us know! In the message, feel free to provide details on the scale of the event you’re interested in, where it would be held, and anything else you feel would be important. We’ll get back to you as soon as we can! It is individuals and organizations (such as yourself) that offer us such opportunities that help us progress our mission of exciting STEM inside of students everywhere!

Become a Partner

Interested in a mutually benefit partnership with Project BEST? We are constantly working with a variety of organizations, both local and national, and would love to work with you as well!
